Our goal is to create a set of educational videos (inspired by Khan Academy) that teach you the basics of playing piano. You'll learn how to play cool classical pieces like Für Elise, and even parts of popular songs (like the intro to Skyfall).

We need your help! Your donation will help us pay for a digital piano. :-) Then we'll start cranking out those tutorials. Donate what you like! (We suggest $5 to $10...)

When you donate, you can also add your email to our mailing list, to get notified when our tutorials are live. You'll also get behind-the-scenes videos!

We'll start off by making 10-20 free videos.

If we hit our goal of $5000, we'll make 100 free videos!

If we hit our stretch goal of $10000, we'll even make an accompanying Piano University app for your phone and tablet!
