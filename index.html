Our goal is to create a set of educational videos (like Khan Academy) that teach you the basics of playing piano. You'll learn how to play cool classical pieces like Für Elise, and even parts of popular songs (e.g., Skyfall).

We need your help! Your donation will help pay for the digital piano and recording equipment. We suggest a donation of $10, but you can donate as much as you like.

We'll also add your email to a mailing list, and notify you when the tutorials are live. You can also choose to follow us on Twitter or Facebook to get updates.

I plan to cover a variety of topics, including note-by-note breakdowns of songs, finger exercises, and music theory.

If we surpass our funding goal, we'll make an accompanying Piano University app for your iPad/iPhone/iPod!

We currently have two free apps on the App Store: Tiny Piano and Tiny Guitar.

FAQ

Will the videos really be free?
Yes. Our lessons will be hosted on our website and YouTube for free.
How will you feed your family if the videos are free?

OK, you got us there. We'll probably add iTunes links from our YouTube videos which will earn a 5% commission every time someone buys a song through our link.

Additionally, we'll put advertisements in our iPad/iPhone apps. You'll be able to upgrade to get premium lessons and remove the advertising.

Who are you, anyways?
I'm just a guy who loves music. I want to teach the world how to play piano! Check out my profile on LinkedIn.
Are you qualified to teach me piano?
I'm not an expert, but I'm currently trying to teach myself piano... and I'd like to share the knowledge I gain with the world. My videos will have to speak for themselves, I guess. :-)
